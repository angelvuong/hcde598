Assignment 1: Laser Cut Press Fit Construction Kit

I created a press kit inspired by concrete breeze block screens that were popularized in American mid-century architecture.

Collaborators:
Connor Stein, M Arch - Thank you for the time you spent helping me learn Rhino and for letting me use your scrap cardboard!
Liang He, CS PhD - Thank you for showing me MakerCase!

Iteration 1-4

I started this project by designing a block layout in Illustrator. I used a template that I generated in MakerCase. I was inspired by the "Empress" breeze block pattern and incorporated it into my design by using simple shapes in Illustrator. Surprisingly, my first iteration turned out well (13% power, 9% speed)! The box stood on its own but collapsed with light taps.
Iteration 2 (14% power,9% speed) used different teeth connectors but had trouble connecting.
Iteration 3 (25% power, 10% speed) could not stand on its own and frequently collapsed.
